OOP : way of designing applications
	1.Encapsulation
		->Unit of information that consists of fields and functions
		->Is a process of enclosing one or more item within a physical or a logical package
		->It prevents access to implementation details using access specifiers
	2.Abstraction 
		->giving away only required data and hiding the rest
	3.Inheritance
		->Extension
	4.Polymorphism
		->Exist in many forms
